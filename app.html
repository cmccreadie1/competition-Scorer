🎣 ANGLER'S QUICK START

Step 1: Join the Match
Enter your Name and the Venue. Tap "Join as Competitor" and type in the 4-digit PIN provided by your Steward.

Step 2: Log Your Catch
Select the fish species. Enter the size or weight, then hit "Record Catch". The app will instantly sync to the cloud.

Step 3: The Dynamic HUD & Leaderboard
The pill at the top of your screen is your tactical HUD. It shows your current points, match timer, and live rank. Tap the pill at any time to unfold the full Live Leaderboard.

Step 4: Smart Battery Saver
There is no sleep button. Put your phone in your pocket and let the screen turn off to save battery. The moment you wake your phone, the app will instantly sync and scroll any new catches across your screen.

👑 STEWARD'S GUIDE

Step 1: Setup
Tap "Host as Steward". Choose the match duration and scoring format, then give your anglers the Room PIN.

Step 2: Management (The Command Bridge)
During the match, Press and Hold the top HUD pill for 1 second to open the Steward Desk. From here, you can adjust the match clock, end the match early, or transfer Steward control to another angler.
