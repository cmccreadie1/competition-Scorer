Interactive Dynamic Hub

Keep your eyes on the surf, not your screen. The 'Hub' is a permanent display pill at the top of the app. It constantly updates to show your live rank, total points, and the match clock. Just wake your phone to see exactly where you stand—no swiping or digging through menus required.

Live Leaderboard

Real-time rankings. See exactly what your mates are catching as it happens. Tap any name to view their individual catch feed in real-time.

Zero Friction Logging

Built for cold, wet hands. Tap once to choose from 80+ pre-populated species. 3-second fish logging with no typing. The app does the math and updates the live leaderboard instantly.

Flexible & Remote Fishing

Mates can fish miles apart on the same live leaderboard. Need to pack up early? Leave the match safely without losing your points, and watch the rest of the battle live from your History tab.

Bulletproof Reliability

Accidentally closed the app? Don't panic. Lock your screen to save power, and if you hit a dead zone, it safely caches your fish and auto-syncs the second you reconnect.

One-Tap WhatsApp Reports

When the clock strikes zero, the app calculates final standings. Generate and share a clean, official match report straight to your group with a single tap.
