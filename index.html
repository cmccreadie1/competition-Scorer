App Specifications

Privacy Guaranteed

This app is 100% Free. We do not use trackers, we do not collect your personal data, and there are zero advertisements.

Friendly Competition

A casual match engine built for friends and club social days where the spirit of angling comes first.

Instant Live Sync

Leaderboards update second-by-second. Includes a Sky Sports style live catch ticker for the ultimate match feel.

Virtual Comps

Not beach specific. Compete against mates on different coastlines or regions simultaneously via a shared PIN.

Low Battery Usage

Engine optimized for minimal power drain. Built to last the duration of long sessions without killing your phone.

Data Resilience

Cloud persistence means your score is untouchable. Your data is safe even if your battery dies or the app is closed.

Flexible Entry

Join late or leave the beach early. Your personal match journal is permanently saved to your local history vault.
