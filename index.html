Engine Features

Dynamic Island HUD

Never lose focus. The permanent tactical pill displays your live rank, total points, and a smart adrenaline clock that shifts formats as the match enters the final 30-minute window.

Battery Management

Designed for full 6-hour sessions. There are no heavy background loops. Lock your phone, save your battery, and the engine will instantly wake up the second you unlock your screen.

Remote Competitions

Anglers can be on different beaches, opposite ends of the country, or even the other side of the world. If they have the Match PIN, they are on the live leaderboard.

Offline Resilience

Built for dead zones. If you lose signal on the beach, the engine safely caches your catches locally and silently syncs the moment you reconnect.

No Ads, Tracking, or Nonsense

This app is 100% private. Unlike Facebook, which is over 500MB and tracks your location, this engine is less than 1MB and physically cannot access your camera, photos, or messages.
