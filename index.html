1. Purpose of the Application

Sea Diary: Match Edition Pro is a specialized, cloud-synchronized platform designed to manage sea angling tournaments in real-time. By moving away from traditional paper scorecards, the app provides instant transparency through a Live Leader Board, automated time management, and a centralized reporting system for Marshalls. It is built to work reliably in coastal environments, offering offline resilience and high-visibility controls for use in direct sunlight.

2. The Marshall’s Perspective (Steward)

As the Steward, you are the "Master of the Room." You control the tournament clock and the scoring parameters.

  • Initialization: Enter your name and venue. Navigate to the Steward section to select match duration and scoring format.
  • PIN System: Creating a session generates a unique 4-digit code. Competitors require this to link.
  • Starting: Once anglers are in the lobby, tap Start Match to trigger a synchronized 5-second countdown on every phone.
  • Ending: Allow clock to reach zero or use "Strike Now" to activate the 5-Minute Grace Period.

3. The Competitor’s Perspective (Angler)

As an Angler, your focus is logging catches accurately and monitoring standings.

  • Joining: Enter name and venue, then provide the PIN from your Marshall.
  • Scorecard: Select species and enter measurement. Entries without size logs are blocked. For unlisted fish, use "[Add Other Species...]" at the bottom of the dropdown.
  • Standings: Tap the trophy to view the Live Leader Board and the "Match Biggest Fish."
  • Grace Period: At expiration, the screen turns Maroon. You have 5 mins to submit captures before locking.
