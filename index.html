🎣 Angler's Quick Start

Step 1: Join the Match
On the front page, enter your Name and the Venue. Tap "Join as Competitor" and type in the 4-digit PIN provided by your Steward.

THE GOLDEN RULE: Tap the icon at the top right until it becomes a . This stops your phone from locking during the match.

Step 2: Log Your Catch
Select the fish species from the list. Enter the size or weight, then hit "Record Catch". Your total will update instantly.

Step 3: The Leaderboard
Tap the big Amber button at the top to see the live standings. You can see who is winning and who has the biggest fish at any time.

Step 4: The Final Whistle
When time runs out, the screen will turn RED. You have 3 minutes to log your final fish before the board locks forever.

RETAINED DATA: If you need to leave early, just tap "Leave Match Early". Your score remains on the Steward's board until the end of the day.

👑 Steward's Guide

Step 1: Setup
Tap "Host as Steward". Choose your match duration and the scoring format (Points, Weight, or Species Hunt).

Step 2: The Lobby
Call out the PIN displayed on your screen. As anglers join, you will see their names appear with a green checkmark.

Step 3: Starting
When everyone is ready, tap "START MATCH". This triggers a 5-second countdown and sounds the horn on everyone's phone simultaneously.

Step 4: Management
Use the "Steward Desk" button at the bottom of the fishing screen to add more time, end the match early, or transfer control to another person.

Step 5: Exporting
When the match ends, tap "Master Export" to share the full final leaderboard to your WhatsApp group or social media.
