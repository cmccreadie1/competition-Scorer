App Specifications

Privacy Guaranteed

This app is 100% Free. We do not use trackers, we do not collect your personal data, and there are zero advertisements.

Instant Live Sync

Leaderboards update second-by-second. Features a Sky Sports style live catch ticker for real-time match updates.

Virtual Competitions

Not beach specific. Compete against friends on different coastlines or regions simultaneously via a shared PIN.

Low Battery Usage

Engine optimized for minimal power drain. Built to last the duration of long sessions without killing your battery.

Data Resilience

Never lose your score. Cloud persistence ensures your data is safe even if your phone dies or you accidentally close the app.

Flexible Match Entry

Dynamic entry for late arrivals. If you leave early, your personal match journal is permanently saved to your local history.

iPhone Installation

1. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari.

2. Scroll down and select 'Add to Home Screen'.

3. Launch from your home screen for the full Pro experience.
