The ultimate live scorecard built for casual beach matches.

Interactive Dynamic Hub

Keep your eyes on the surf, not your screen. The 'Hub' is a permanent display pill at the top of the app. It constantly updates to show your live rank, total points, and the match clock. Just wake your phone to see exactly where you stand—no swiping or digging through menus required.

Live Leaderboard

See exactly who is catching what, and how many points you need to steal the lead. Tap anyone's name to instantly view their catch diary as it happens.

Zero Friction Logging

Built for cold, wet hands. Tap once to choose from 80+ pre-populated species, neatly broken down by Commonly Caught and Fish Categories. It is 3-second fish logging with no typing and zero searching. The app does the math and updates the live leader board instantly.

Flexible & Remote Fishing

You don't have to be on the same stretch of sand to compete. Mates can fish miles apart on the same live leaderboard. Need to pack up early? Leave the match safely without losing your points, and watch the rest of the battle live from your History tab on the drive home.

Bulletproof Reliability

Accidentally closed the app? Don't panic. You can't lose your place or your data. The engine uses practically zero battery in the background. Lock your screen to save power, and if you hit a dead zone, it safely caches your fish and auto-syncs the second you reconnect.

One-Tap WhatsApp Results

When the clock strikes zero, the app calculates the final standings and total beach stats. Generate and share a clean, official match report straight to your WhatsApp group with a single tap.
